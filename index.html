Adding books

  • Files / iCloud Drive: tap Share › BookHaven, or open BookHaven and tap +.
  • AirDrop from another device — pick BookHaven as the destination.
  • Cloud services (Dropbox, Google Drive, OneDrive): connect via Discover › Connect a cloud service, then browse and import.
  • Email or Safari downloads: open the attachment, Share › BookHaven.

Supported formats

EPUB, PDF, MOBI, AZW3, FB2, TXT, RTF for ebooks. M4B, M4A, MP3 for audiobooks. DRM-protected files don't work — Kindle, Audible, and DRM'd Apple Books need to be DRM-free first.

My Kindle books?

Amazon's DRM means BookHaven can't read Kindle files directly. If you've got DRM-free EPUBs of books you own, drop them in via the methods above.

Audiobooks

M4B, M4A, MP3 — single files or folders of chapters (including disc-folder layouts like CD 1, CD 2). Dolby Atmos plays in spatial audio on AirPods Pro/Max and HomePod. CarPlay support comes after launch.

iCloud sync (Beta)

Off by default in v1.0. Turn it on in Settings › iCloud Sync (Beta) to share library, bookmarks, and progress across your Apple devices. Audiobook files don't sync — re-import them from cloud services on the other device.

Subscription vs premium rooms

Two independent purchase types — buying one does NOT buy the other:

  • Ad-Free Membership — subscription that removes ads. Doesn't unlock rooms.
  • Premium room purchases — one-time per room (Tower, Nook, Zen Garden, Witch's Attic). Unlocks the room. Doesn't remove ads.

The Study is free forever. New users get a 7-day welcome gift: every haven unlocked + ads off.

Refunds

App Store refunds are handled by Apple at reportaproblem.apple.com. If a purchase didn't unlock what you expected, write to us first — most "didn't unlock" confusion is about which axis (subscription vs room) does what.

Privacy

  • No servers. Your library and progress live on your device.
  • iCloud sync is private to your Apple ID. We don't see it.
  • Free-tier ads come from Google AdMob. AdMob doesn't receive your reading data.
  • No analytics, no behavioral tracking, no account.

Full policy: hcyalcin.github.io/bookhaven-privacy
